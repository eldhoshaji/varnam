About Varnam

With over 30 events spread over six days, Varnam aims to create a time of ethereal bliss for all its patreons. With a wide variety of events to choose from, spread over 10 modules, patreons of the fest will always have a great time taking part in or viewing the various events of their choice and are sure to experince the 'Eternal Dream Time' while in Varnam.
